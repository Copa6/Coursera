Come Fly with us!

If riding in an airplane is flying, then riding in a boat is swimming. If you want to experience the element, then get out of the vehicle.

Why Fly With Us


How do you clear your head? A relaxing bath? A strong cup of coffee? A brisk walk? How about jumping out of an aeroplane at 10,000 metres? If you’re looking for an adrenalin rush, a feeling of freedom and an incredible sense of accomplishment, pretty much nothing on earth (or in the sky) beats skydiving.

It's easier than you think

Your first skydive will be a tandem jump, which means you’re strapped to an experienced instructor and you dive together. Not only does this mean you don’t have to worry about making any mistakes, it also means he or she will be doing all the hard work. A 7,000-foot tandem skydive will set you back around £150 and gives you 10 seconds of freefall before the chute opens.

Conquering your fears gives you confidance

Nothing seems more unnatural than jumping out of an aeroplane one and a half miles above the earth. But that’s exactly why you should do it. Overcoming such a hurdle means confronting your fears – and then facing them down and doing it anyway. It will scare the living daylights out of you, but you will never feel more empowered.

Your friends will be insanely jealous

Let’s face it: you’re going to get the mother of all selfies up there. And the best Monday-morning “so did you get up to anything interesting at the weekend?” story the office will have heard in a long time.
